Favourite Movies

My top 3 Favourite Movies

Dune

Dune is an overall exciting movie. It has many characters that are very mysterious and intresting.The landscape and enviroment is also very exciting because it consists of deathworms creatures that live in the desert. They prevented them from mining special spices from the sand and worked together to defeat the deathworms.

Holes

Holes is a intresting movie about a kid named stanley who has a long curse set on his family of bad luck. He is always in the wrong place at the wrong time amd nothing ever works out in their family. He is sent to a dried up lake to dig holes as a punishment for stealing some shoes. There he suffers in the heat digging holes and becomes freinds with a boy named zero. Eventually they end up running away and finding a mountain next to the dried up lake. They find water and food up there and while carrying zero up the mountain he breaks the curse that was on his family. Then they go back to camp and get taken home after finding treasure buried in the lake on their way back.

Hacksaw Ridge

Hacksaw ridge is about a christian man that goes to war and gets beaten for his faith and for refusing to take up arms. He prevails and goes to war eventually without a gun but as a medic. There he saves many lives and shows the power of his faith. Towards the end he got many of the men that beat him and shamed him to turn to faith.
